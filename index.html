About Us

EduLight is a modern education partner delivering secure, curriculum-aligned learning support for students across Nigeria. We combine a mobile-ready digital platform with expert one-on-one tutoring to help learners achieve measurable improvement.

Our team builds structured lessons, progress tracking tools, and personalized coaching so every child can learn with confidence, focus, and flexibility.

We work with families, schools, and communities to remove barriers, lower education costs, and provide a safer, more effective route to academic success.
